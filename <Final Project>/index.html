Welcome to Game-Plan

Are you hopelessy indecisive when it comes to choosing what to eat? What to watch on Netflix? What game to play, or book to read? If you can't make up your mind, and want to let The Fates decide for you, Game-Plan is a web application that will randomly pick one of your options for you. Give it a try below!

Game-Plan: Made in New York City (2018)
